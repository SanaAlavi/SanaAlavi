## ♨️ About Me

I’m a Computer Engineering student with a deep passion for *building things that bridge hardware and imagination.  
I shipped two indie games using C# and Unity, and that experience taught me how to think in systems – from game loops to GPU pipelines.  
Now I’m diving into Data Science & AI, fascinated by the math and the neural architectures that power them.  
Simultaneously, I’m obsessed with computer architecture – understanding how silicon turns into logic, and logic into experience.  
Currently mastering MySQL to solidify my database foundations, and this summer I’m going full‑stack with PHP, JavaScript & SQL to create dynamic websites that actually do something.  
My dream? To become the engineer who can design a chip, train a model on it, and ship the full product – game, website, or AI app.  
I’m not just learning to code; I’m learning to craft worlds.*
